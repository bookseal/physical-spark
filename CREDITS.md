## Design inspiration
- The visual mood is inspired by *Re-Volt* (1999, Acclaim Entertainment). **No Re-Volt assets are shipped.**
  Its reference screenshots live in `design/_refs/rv-*`, which is gitignored — they exist only on a local
  checkout, and the published [moodboard](design/moodboard.html) renders a "local only" placeholder in their
  place rather than a broken image.
- Everything else in `design/_refs/` **is** ours and does ship: the AI-generated art (`gen-*.webp`, the
  founder's own) and the generated videos (`vid-*.mp4`). The heavy PNG originals of the generated art are
  gitignored (`gen-*.png`); only the webp versions are published.
