② Prompt template (fill the [slots])

A [tiny toy RC car / small toy robot arm], [action: slowly rolling / idling / gently waving], through a giant [ENVIRONMENT: sunlit toy-store aisle / kitchen table / kid's bedroom floor scattered with toys / botanical garden], shot in [tilt-shift macro] so the real world looks like a toy set. [Late-90s toy-commercial] mood, warm golden-hour light, shallow depth of field, soft bokeh. Camera: [very slow dolly-in / static with gentle parallax]. Seamless loop, 16:9, no text, lots of empty space in the [upper-left] for a headline. Playful but premium — plastic and bright, never cheap.

③ Tips (so the video actually works as a hero)

  • Tilt-shift / macro is the whole trick — it makes a real scene read as a toy world (that's the Re-Volt feeling).
  • Subtle motion only. A hero behind text must not distract: slow dolly, idle sway, dust motes. Ask for seamless loop.
  • Negative space — tell it to leave a clear area for your headline (e.g. upper-left).
  • No baked-in text in the generation. Add your wordmark in HTML/CSS on top.
  • Veo (Gemini): describe camera + lighting + loop. Sora (ChatGPT): similar; add duration (e.g. "4-second loop").
  • Generate a few, keep the calmest. 80/20 rule: the hero is the 20% playful — don't overcook it.
